goBlue

goBlue is an app-based business with a mission to help protect and conserve South Florida's marinelife and coastlines, while at the same time teaching beach-goers and everyone else how to protect our oceans. goBlue was made for the Loggerhead Marinelife Center in Juno Beach, FL. The application is used to connect people and nature, as well as give them the information needed to protect it. Our oceans aren't going to clean themselves, so everyone must help and goBlue.

Cabana Health

The Cabana Health iOS app syncs wellness data to allow users with similar health goals to form teams called “cabanas”. I was an intern here for my fall 2020 semester, where I helped develop the Explore and sign-in pages, as well as scaled the Firebase backend system to achieve a quicker user experience with the app.
