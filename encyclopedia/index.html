Surfing

Surfing is a surface water sport in which an individual, a surfer uses a board to ride a wave in the ocean. The propulsion from the wave will allow the surfer to ride on the wave at certain speeds depending on where on the face of the wave the surfer is located. As the face of the wave grows larger and starts to barrel, it is common for surfers to crouch and get inside of the barrel given they are at certain speeds. It is possible to surf in a tidal bore, or in wave pools (See picture on the right).
